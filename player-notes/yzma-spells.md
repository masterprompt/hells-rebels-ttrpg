# Yzma Tennesan - Spells Known

**Class**: Sorcerer 17 (Gold Dragon Bloodline - Fire)
**Bloodline Arcana**: Fire spells deal +1 damage per die rolled.
**Permanent Effects**: Arcane Sight (always active)

Bloodline spells marked with (B). These are granted automatically by the Gold Dragon bloodline and do not count against spells known.
